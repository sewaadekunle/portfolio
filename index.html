About Me

Turning numbers
into narratives

Hi, I am Sewa, a data analyst with a bachelor's degree in Statistics from Obafemi Awolowo University. My interest in data grew through university projects and real-world analytics roles, where I found that the most valuable thing an analyst can do is not just process numbers, but uncover the story they tell.

I work with businesses and teams to make sense of their data. Whether it is understanding what is driving revenue, identifying what is underperforming, or spotting patterns that are not immediately obvious, I focus on translating data into clear, decision-ready insights.

I also co-authored an academic research paper applying statistical modeling to socio-demographic data, which sharpened my ability to approach complex datasets with structure and rigour.
